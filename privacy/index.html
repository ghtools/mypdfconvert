Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Files never leave your device

Every tool on MyPDFConvert runs entirely in your web browser using JavaScript. When you choose a PDF or image file, it is processed locally on your device. At no point is your file uploaded, transmitted, or stored on any server we operate. We simply have no access to the contents of any file you process.

What information we do collect

Like most websites, we use standard analytics to understand how many people visit the site and which pages are popular. This may include your approximate location (based on IP address), browser type, and device type. This data is aggregated and is not linked to the files you process.

Advertising

We display ads through a third-party advertising network (Adsterra) to keep MyPDFConvert free. These networks may use cookies or similar technologies to serve relevant ads. We do not control how third-party ad networks use this data; please refer to their own privacy policies for details.

Cookies

We do not use cookies to track your activity across other websites. Any cookies present on this site are set by our advertising partners for the purpose of serving ads.

Children's privacy

MyPDFConvert is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the revised policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent via the contact information listed at the bottom of the homepage.
