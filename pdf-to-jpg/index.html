Frequently Asked Questions

What do I get if my PDF has multiple pages?

Each page is converted into its own JPG image, packaged together in a single ZIP file for download.

What if my PDF only has one page?

You'll get a single JPG file directly — no ZIP needed.

What resolution are the images?

Pages are rendered at 2x scale for sharp, high-quality images suitable for viewing or printing.

Is my PDF uploaded anywhere to convert it?

No. The conversion happens entirely in your browser — nothing is uploaded to a server.
