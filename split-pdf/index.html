Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between the two split modes?

"Split every page" breaks the PDF into one file per page, packaged as a ZIP. "Extract a page range" pulls out a continuous range (like pages 3 to 7) into a single new PDF.

Can I split a PDF into individual pages?

Yes. Choose "Split every page into a separate PDF" and every page becomes its own file, delivered as a ZIP download.

Will splitting affect the quality of my pages?

No. Splitting just separates the existing pages — there's no re-compression or quality change.

Can I extract just a few specific pages, not a continuous range?

For a continuous range, use Split PDF. To pick specific, non-continuous pages (like 1, 4, and 9), use the Extract Pages tool instead.
