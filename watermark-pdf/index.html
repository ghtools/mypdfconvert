Frequently Asked Questions

Can I choose where the watermark appears?

Yes. Choose from center (diagonal or horizontal), or any of the four corners.

Can I change the watermark text?

Yes. Type any text you like — common examples are "CONFIDENTIAL", "DRAFT", or a company name.

Can I change the color?

Yes. Choose gray, red, blue, or black, and adjust the opacity to make it subtle or bold.

Does the watermark appear on every page?

Yes, the same watermark is applied to every page of the document.
