Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change the watermark text?

Yes. Type any text you like — common examples are "CONFIDENTIAL", "DRAFT", or a company name — and it will be stamped diagonally across every page.

Can I control how visible the watermark is?

Yes. Use the opacity setting to make the watermark subtle and light, or bold and clearly visible.

Does the watermark appear on every page?

Yes, the same watermark is applied to every page of the document.

Can I remove a watermark I've already added?

Not with this tool — once a watermark is stamped onto the page content, it becomes part of the page itself and can't be automatically removed.
