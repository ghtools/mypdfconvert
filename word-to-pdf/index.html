Frequently Asked Questions

Will my formatting, images, and tables be preserved?

No. This tool extracts the text from your Word document and lays it out as plain paragraphs in a new PDF. Fonts, images, tables, and complex layout are not preserved.

What file types are supported?

Only .docx files (the modern Word format) are supported. Older .doc files are not supported.

Why isn't full formatting supported?

Accurately recreating a Word document's exact layout requires heavy server-side processing. To keep this tool free, private, and running entirely in your browser, we focus on reliably converting the text instead.

Is my document uploaded anywhere?

No. The conversion happens entirely in your browser — your file is never uploaded to a server.
