Frequently Asked Questions

Can I combine multiple images into one PDF?

Yes. Select as many JPG or PNG images as you like — each one becomes its own page in the final PDF, in the order you arrange them.

Can I change the order of the images?

Yes. After selecting your images, drag and drop them in the list to set the page order before converting.

Does this work with PNG files too?

Yes. Both JPG and PNG images are supported and can be mixed in the same PDF.

Will the image quality be reduced?

No. Each image is placed into the PDF at its original resolution, with no re-compression.
