Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enter which pages to remove?

Type page numbers separated by commas, and use a hyphen for a range. For example, "2, 5-7, 10" removes page 2, pages 5 through 7, and page 10.

Can I remove every page from the PDF?

No, at least one page must remain in the resulting PDF.

Will the remaining pages keep their original order?

Yes. The pages you don't remove stay in the same order as the original document.

What if I want to keep only a few specific pages instead?

Use the Extract Pages tool instead — it lets you choose exactly which pages to keep rather than which to remove.
