Frequently Asked Questions

Can I merge more than two PDFs at once?

Yes. You can select as many PDF files as you like — add them one at a time or all together, then reorder them before merging.

Can I change the order of the pages before merging?

Yes. After choosing your files, drag and drop them in the list to set the order they'll appear in the final PDF.

Will merging reduce the quality of my PDFs?

No. Merging simply combines the original pages as-is, with no re-compression or quality loss.

Is there a limit to how many files I can merge?

There's no fixed limit — the only constraint is your device's available memory, since everything runs in your browser.
