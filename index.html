Frequently Asked Questions

Is MyPDFConvert really free?

Yes. Every tool on MyPDFConvert is completely free to use, with no hidden charges, watermarks, or usage limits.

Do I need to sign up or create an account?

No. You can use every tool immediately without creating an account or providing an email address.

Are my files uploaded to a server?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your files never leave your device.

Is it safe to use MyPDFConvert for sensitive documents?

Yes. Because files are processed entirely in your browser and never uploaded anywhere, your documents stay private.

Does MyPDFConvert work on mobile phones?

Yes. MyPDFConvert is fully responsive and works on Android, iPhone, and tablets, in addition to desktop browsers.

What is the maximum file size I can process?

Since files are processed in your browser, the limit depends on your device's available memory rather than a fixed cap. Most documents work smoothly.

Can I merge PDFs from different devices?

You can merge any PDF files you can select from your device's file picker, regardless of where they originally came from.

Will compressing a PDF reduce its quality?

Compression re-encodes page images at a chosen quality level, so there is a small quality trade-off for a smaller file size. You can choose a lower compression level to preserve more quality.

Do I need to install any software?

No installation is required. MyPDFConvert works entirely inside your web browser.

Which browsers are supported?

MyPDFConvert works on all modern browsers, including Chrome, Safari, Firefox, and Edge, on both desktop and mobile.
